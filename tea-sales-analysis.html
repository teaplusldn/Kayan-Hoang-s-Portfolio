Tea+ Sales Data Optimization

Project Objective

The aim of this project was to streamline daily operations and improve inventory management at Tea+ by analyzing sales data. Data-driven techniques were applied to reduce waste and optimize stock levels.

Skills Applied

  • Data Analysis with Python (pandas, matplotlib)
  • Sales Trend Forecasting
  • Inventory Optimization

Key Achievements

By analyzing sales trends, I identified inefficiencies in stock management, which allowed me to reduce inventory waste by 15%. These optimizations improved operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.
